Our program coderafiki aims at at forming a network of after school clubs made up of a partnership between students, volunteer high skilled developers and schools. The aim of this project is to bridge the gap which is the dissemination of Information Technology education to schools.

How we are making
a diffrence

Kenya and Africa are in need for knowledge and awareness about the importance of IT and most importantly coding.

A very small population in the continent is equipped with knowledge of coding and emerging technologies such as web3, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and Virtual Reality work.Our aim is to change this by creating more awareness about the coding and opportiunities in the IT sector. Our target group is school going children between the ages of 10-19 years.Studies have shown that teaching students how to code at an early stage is considered as one of the major keys to promoting engineering education in the future, which can potentially enhance the competency and computational skills of students to adhere better learning outcomes in higher education.
