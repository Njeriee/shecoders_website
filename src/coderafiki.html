About coderafiki

Coderafiki initiative is a an afterschool program designed to teach school going children at the primary and highschool level how to code. The projects focuses on traning the students on key aspects used when coding such as problem solving, creativity , team work and attention to detail.At the advanced levels students get learn advanced concepts such as

  • Web3 development
  • Blockchain development
  • Airtifical Intelligence
  • Virtual reality
  • Game development

Why coderafiki

Our aim

Our main objective is to impart basic knowledge of how to code and use coding as a tool for problem solving in the community.

Our objectives

  • To create a trend of curiosity for computer programming and careers available in the IT space
  • To collaborate with schools in order to form after school coding clubs.
  • To offer quality guidance and mentorship to students interested in learning how to code
  • To identify challenges faced by students when accessing IT information and materials for practice
  • To determine the impact of early introduction to coding for students
