About us

Who we are

We are a group of young kenyans who are driven and passionate about transoforming our communities through Information Technology. We seek to create awareness and inspire the young generation about vast world of information technolgy and how they can position themselves to learn and create products that will solve day to day challanges in our communitues, our country , our continent and the world at large.
