Who are shecoders

We are a group of young ladies in the IT space.We are the founders of the coderafiki project , a program aimed at bridging the gap of disseminating coding education to schools. We are transforming our society through spreading awareness about opportiunities in IT.

What do shechoders do
  • We offer basic and advanced coding classes for children between the ages of 10-19ys
  • We design and develop both web and mobile applications as a sustainability measure of coderafiki project
  • We are champions of emerging technologies such as web3, blockchain,virtual reality and Artificial Intelligence
How can I get involved and help shecoders

You can join the coderafiki project as a volunteer developer or as a donor supporting the cause.
